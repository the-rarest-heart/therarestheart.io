Twelve years as a professional athlete. Discipline, performance under pressure, and the understanding that preparation determines outcomes. Technology was always a passion — but a formal path never came.

// chapter 02 — the technician

A new career in IT. Expected to perform at a level he hadn't yet fully trained for. The systems were real, the tickets were live, and the pressure to know — without showing what you didn't know — was constant.

// chapter 03 — the builder

So he built the answer. TheGERD was created to solve the exact problem he was living: how do I access the right knowledge, right now, without sending sensitive data to the cloud? Self-taught, certified, and shipping.

Founded The Rarest Heart because that is who he is — the person who shows up and helps, even when it is hard. Technology should close gaps, not widen them.
